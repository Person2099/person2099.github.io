# about

About me
I'm a Computer Science student based in Melbourne, Australia, with a strong interest in technology, systems, and problem solving. I enjoy working across both software and hardware, from building applications to troubleshooting real-world technical issues.

I spend a lot of time experimenting with different technologies, whether that's writing small apps, automating tasks, or troubleshooting hardware and operating systems. I've worked with macOS, Windows, Linux, and mobile platforms, and I enjoy learning how large systems are designed, supported, and improved over time.

I like working on practical projects, including mobile applications, automation scripts, and hardware repairs, and I'm always looking for opportunities to expand my technical skills and take on new challenges. My goal is to build a career in technology where I can contribute to systems that have real impact on people's everyday lives.

Right now I'm especially interested in cloud, networking, and backend systems, and I'm always looking for new projects to learn from.
